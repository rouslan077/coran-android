# 📖 Quran — Read and listen, simply

A **free** Android app to read and listen to the Quran: Arabic text (Classic or Uthmani script), transliteration, English, French and Russian translations, and verse-by-verse synchronized audio recitations. The app automatically follows your phone's language (English, French or Russian).

## ⬇️ Download

**[📥 Download the APK (latest version)](https://github.com/rouslan077/coran-android/releases/latest)**

### Installation
1. Download the `.apk` file on your Android phone.
2. Open it and accept the installation (allow "Install unknown apps" if prompted).
3. Open the app and start reading.

## ✨ Features

- **Complete reading**: all 114 surahs, with transliteration and translation.
- **Synchronized listening**: the audio follows the current verse — English (Ibrahim Walk), French, Russian and Arabic (Mishary Rashid Alafasy) reciters.
- **Offline audio**: download surahs and listen without a connection.
- **Resume reading**: pick up exactly where you left off.
- **Favorites and custom folders**, verse annotations.
- **Light / dark theme**, typography and customizable settings.
